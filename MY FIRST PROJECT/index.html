"Throughout heaven and earth, I alone am the honored one" - Satoru Gojo uses this quote to emphasize his unparalleled strength and status as the strongest sorcerer in the Jujutsu Kaisen series.

The million dollar question remains, "Are you the Strongest because you're Satoru Gojo? Or Are You Satoru Gojo Because You're the Strongest?" as asked by his best friend Suguru Geto as he leaves to find a purporse in his life far from being a Jujutsu sorcerer.
